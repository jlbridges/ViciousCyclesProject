At Vicious Cycles, our mission is to welcome new and old riders to the freedom and speed of the bike.

Customer Satisfaction: We don’t let people walk away unhappy, even if you don’t purchase a bike today, you are still part of our community.

Staff: Our staff are the bread and butter of Vicious Cycles. They are knowledgeable and passionate about our products and are here to serve our customers and communities.

The Ride: The ride is not something that can be put into words, you have to experience it for yourself. There is nothing quite like the speed and freedom the bike provides while knowing each bike has a story.

Each bike tells a story; come in today to experience the character each bike offers.
